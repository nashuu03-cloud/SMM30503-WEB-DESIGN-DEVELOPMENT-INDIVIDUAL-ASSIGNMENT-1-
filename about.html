ABOUT ME

Muhammad Amirul Aiman bin Mohamad Zaini is a young veterinary professional from Perlis. He studied at the Malaysian Veterinary Institute in Kluang, Johor, and earned the Malaysian Skills Certificate (SKM Level 3) in Animal Veterinary Science after SPM. Just one month after graduation, he started working at the Department of Veterinary Services in Perlis, his hometown. At first, he worked as a Veterinary Assistant, taking care of farms and animal health. With two years of experience, he was later promoted to the livestock slaughtering department. Now at 23 years old, Amirul continues to build his career in veterinary services, showing strong dedication and passion for animal health and welfare.
